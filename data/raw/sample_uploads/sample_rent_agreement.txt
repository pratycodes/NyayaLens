Residential Rent Agreement

This Rent Agreement is made on 15 June 2025 between Green Homes LLP as landlord and Arjun Mehta as tenant for the premises at Indiranagar, Bengaluru, Karnataka.

Monthly Rent: The tenant shall pay monthly rent of INR 35,000 on or before the 5th day of each month.

Security Deposit: The tenant has paid a security deposit equal to 2 months rent, being INR 70,000, refundable after adjustment of unpaid dues and documented damages.

Lock-in Period: The tenancy has a lock-in period of 6 months from the start date.

Notice Period: Either party may terminate the tenancy by giving 30 days written notice after the lock-in period.

Repairs and Maintenance: Minor repairs caused by tenant use shall be borne by the tenant. Structural repairs shall be handled by the landlord.

Painting and Cleaning Charges: The landlord may deduct reasonable painting or cleaning charges if supported by bills or agreed move-out condition notes.

Eviction: The landlord may seek vacation of the premises for non-payment of rent or serious breach after written notice.

Police Verification: The tenant shall cooperate with tenant police verification if required locally.

Jurisdiction: Courts at Bengaluru shall have jurisdiction.
