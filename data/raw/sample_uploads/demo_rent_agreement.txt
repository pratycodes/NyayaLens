DEMO RENT AGREEMENT

This rent agreement is made at Demo City between Demo Landlord as Landlord and Demo Tenant as Tenant.
The landlord lets out the rental premises to the tenant for residential use.

Monthly rent is Rs. 30,000 payable by the fifth day of each month.
The tenant has paid a refundable security deposit equal to two months rent.
The agreement has a lock-in period of six months.
Either party must give 30 days written notice before termination after the lock-in period.
The landlord may deduct repair charges from the security deposit only with itemized bills and photos.
Painting or cleaning charges must be supported by the rent agreement and actual bills.
Eviction or demand to vacate must be communicated in writing.
Courts at Demo City shall have jurisdiction.
