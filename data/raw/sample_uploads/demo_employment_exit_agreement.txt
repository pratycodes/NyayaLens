DEMO EMPLOYMENT EXIT AGREEMENT

This sample employment document is between Acme Demo Services LLP as Employer and Demo Employee as Employee.
The Employee is appointed in Demo City.

The Employee must serve a notice period of 90 days before resignation unless waived in writing.
The Employee agrees to a training bond of Rs. 2,00,000 if they resign within 12 months of joining.
Training cost recovery must be supported by written calculation from the Employer.
Full-and-final settlement may be processed after return of company property and completion of exit formalities.
The Employer may not withhold salary without a written calculation and clause reference.
The Employee shall not compete with the Employer for 12 months after exit.
Confidential information must remain confidential after employment ends.
Disputes shall be referred to arbitration in Demo City.
Courts at Demo City shall have jurisdiction.
